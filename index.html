FRC TEAMS IN TORONTO

ABOUT US

As a rookie FRC team composed mainly of immigrants in Toronto, our mission is to increase STEM+ awareness within our community through the encouragement of engaging in an FRC program, and outreach. We also wish to inspire other schools and communities to start their very own FRC teams by showing them how our team was able to make ends meet.

About FRC

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is a high school robotics competition that combines the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. Teams of students, guided by mentors, design, build, and program robots to compete in a themed game challenge. FRC fosters teamwork, innovation, and critical thinking skills as participants solve real-world engineering problems in a fast-paced, competitive environment. It offers students an immersive experience in STEM fields, preparing them for future careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
